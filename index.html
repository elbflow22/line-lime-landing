Questions

Before you sign up.

Is this just another AI script generator?
No. Generation is one small feature. The value is the archive, the editing experience, the style guide it learns from your edits, and the loop connecting what you post to what you write next. If a better AI model appeared tomorrow, Line Lime would get better, not redundant.
Who is Line Lime for?
Professional and semi-professional short-form creators who post often and treat content as their work — plus small content teams. If you're shipping regularly and consistency is your real bottleneck, it's built for you.
Which platforms does it support?
Short-form first — TikTok, Instagram Reels and YouTube Shorts — with long-form YouTube as a secondary format. Your voice can be tuned per platform, because they genuinely aren't interchangeable.

What will it cost?
No credits, ever — unlimited drafting. We're finalizing pricing now, so you'll see your full founding offer at launch before you commit to anything.
