## What you're reserving
A Founding Member slot in Line Lime at the founding rate of **€99 / month, locked
for life** — limited to the first **100 members**, first come, first served.
Reserve before the **Summer Launch** and your founding price is locked forever.
Everyone who joins after launch pays the regular launch price.

## How the reservation works — you are **not** charged today
- You reserve your slot by putting a payment method on file now. **No charge is made
  when you reserve.**
- You are charged your first **€99 only at the Summer Launch — 22 September 2026**
  (the last day of summer), when Line Lime opens and your access begins. Never before.
- Reservations are capped at 100 and filled in order.

## The build condition (why this is safe for you)
- We will only build and launch if at least **10 founding members** reserve by the
  **Summer Launch, 22 September 2026**.
- If we do **not** reach 10 by then, the project doesn't proceed: **no card is ever
  charged**, and every reservation is cancelled automatically. You pay nothing.

## Cancel anytime before launch — free
You can cancel your reservation at any time before the Summer Launch, at no cost.
Nothing has been charged, so there's nothing to refund.

## Your 30-day money-back guarantee (after launch)
- When you're first charged (at the Summer Launch) and get access, you have **30 days**
  to try Line Lime.
- Not happy, for any reason? Request a refund within 30 days for a **full,
  no-questions-asked refund**, and your membership ends.

## After the first 30 days
- If you don't request a refund within 30 days, your Founding membership
  **continues at €99 / month, locked for life.**
- You can cancel anytime, effective at the end of your current billing month.
- *(You may be offered a discounted annual plan.)*

## Timeline
Launch is targeted for the **Summer Launch — 22 September 2026** (the last day of
summer). We'll keep you updated by email. Because you're only charged at launch, a
change in timing never charges you early.

## Your data & payments
- We collect your email (to notify you) and, optionally, a few answers (to build the
  right product).
- Card details are handled entirely by **Stripe** and are never stored by us.
