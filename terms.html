1. Who you're contracting with

Riverland International GmbH, Hangstraße 11, 21075 Hamburg, Germany (HRB 187138, Amtsgericht Hamburg; VAT DE369591654), office@riverland-int.com — the provider of Line Lime (“we”, “us”). See our Impressum.

2. What you're reserving

A Founding Member place in Line Lime at the founding rate of €99 per month, locked for life, limited to the first 100 members (first come, first served). Reserve before the Summer Launch and this rate is yours for as long as your membership runs continuously. Anyone who joins after launch pays the regular launch price.

3. Reservation & payment

  • You reserve by placing a payment method on file via our payment provider Stripe. No payment is taken when you reserve.
  • You are charged your first €99 only at the Summer Launch — 22 September 2026, when Line Lime opens and your access begins. Your membership then renews monthly at €99 until cancelled.
  • You may cancel your reservation free of charge at any time before launch. As nothing has been charged, there is nothing to refund.

4. If we don't launch

We will only launch if at least 10 founding members reserve by 22 September 2026. If we do not reach that, the project does not proceed: no card is ever charged and every reservation is cancelled automatically. You pay nothing.

5. Your right of withdrawal (14 days)

As a consumer you have a statutory right to withdraw from this contract within 14 days without giving reasons. The withdrawal instructions and model form are set out in the German version of these terms (section “Widerrufsbelehrung”), which is legally authoritative. In short: email office@riverland-int.com within 14 days and we refund any payment made, using the same payment method, within 14 days.

On top of the statutory minimum, we give you more:

• Free cancellation any time before launch — nothing has been charged.
• A 30-day, no-questions-asked money-back guarantee after you are first charged at launch: if you're not happy for any reason, email us within 30 days for a full refund and your membership ends.

6. Term & ongoing cancellation

After launch the membership is a monthly subscription. You can cancel at any time with effect from the end of your current billing month; you keep access until then. Cancellation is possible by email or via your account.

7. Prices & VAT

€99 per month. Prices include statutory VAT where applicable; the VAT actually charged depends on your country of residence and is shown at checkout.

8. Governing law

German law applies. Mandatory consumer-protection provisions of your country of residence remain unaffected. We are not obliged, and not willing, to participate in dispute-resolution proceedings before a consumer arbitration board.
