3. What we collect, and why

Reserving a founding spot (your email)

When you reserve a founding spot, we collect your email address so we can notify you about early access and manage the founding list. Legal basis: your consent and steps taken at your request prior to a contract (Art. 6(1)(a) and (b) GDPR). This is stored in our database at Supabase (Supabase, Inc.), in an EU region (Frankfurt, Germany).

Payment details

To reserve, a payment method is placed on file via Stripe. Your card details are entered on Stripe's secure checkout and are processed and stored by Stripe — we never see or store your full card data. Provider: Stripe Payments Europe, Ltd. (Ireland) and Stripe, Inc. (USA). Legal basis: Art. 6(1)(b) GDPR. See Stripe's privacy policy.

Optional questions

If you choose to answer our few optional questions (e.g. which platforms you post on), those answers are sent via Formspree (Formspree, Inc., USA) so we can build the right product. Providing them is voluntary. Legal basis: your consent (Art. 6(1)(a) GDPR).

Web fonts

Fonts are self-hosted on our own server — they are not loaded from Google or any third party, so no font-related data is sent to external providers.

4. Local storage (no tracking)

We store small technical flags in your browser (local/session storage) to remember whether you've already interacted, so we don't repeat the same prompt. These are strictly functional. We currently use no advertising or analytics tracking cookies.
